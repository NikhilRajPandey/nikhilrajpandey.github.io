Why And How You Should Avoid Youtube

12 Sep 2022

In this blog I will tell you about how I avoid youtube and how you should to. Listen I am not telling you to not watch your favourite videos on the youtube (like comedy,music). Its just a particular way don't have to use youtube. This blog is a extension of bugswriter video How to block all internet distractions / ads / malware / crap and Social Dilenema Series

First let me start with what is the problem with normal youtube.

First Reason

Whenever you open youtube It will show all the tempting videos on their homepage. I am not saying that these videos are tempting because I am addicted to them abviously No!. Understand the reason. Youtube track our each and every activity. How much we scroll ? how much we spent time on each video ? At which video thumnail our mouse cursor go ? Yeah I am serious. And based on this they will predict by machine learning algorithms which type of video you are going to watch and find tempting. Now you will tell me whats the problem In this thing ?

Well Here is it. The algorithm will get better and better about knowing your choices and after some time they will be very smart enough to keep you on the youtube. And Trust me this is a very serious issue and If you don't belive me then watch Social Dilenema.

Even when you open youtube in Private Window of Firefox even then it puts shorts (about some sigma boy after rejection) that is tempting for most people (not only for me) and I know that I hate that short but I end up watching like most of the people.

Summary: You can't defeat a algorithm in long run which is running on a super computer.

Second

Second Big reason is they can manipulate you very easily. Let me give you example by telling you my story. I never liked politics/social video But one day youtube recommended this Video about Sugar Mafias.

So I clicked on it and watch the whole video and next day youtube again recommended me video of that channel about One Child trend in India.

and I end up watching that and started interested in political video. Now the whole point of the story is that youtube algorithm manipulated me by making me interested in politics that is not a harm but if youtube algorithm had recommended me some fakenews or wrong type of content I end up getting manuplated.

Third

Third Big reason is the unlimited dose of high dopamine content. I think I don't have to explain after youtube become tiktok. Solutions
